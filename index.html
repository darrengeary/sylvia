What I Offer

We will all face struggles at some stage in life, often managing with support of family and friends. Sometimes we can feel overwhelmed with life issues and may choose to talk with a counsellor.

I am a Humanistic Integrative Psychotherapist, drawing on the Person-Centred Approach as the core of my work. This means I take a holistic view to include your emotional, behavioural, cognitive, and physiological needs. From this perspective I integrate various therapeutic approaches to provide best therapeutic outcome for you.

Types of Therapy

Cognitive Behaviour Therapy (CBT)

Explores thought patterns which in turn affects how you feel and ultimately how you behave.

Learn More
Psychodynamic Therapy

Explores your early childhood and how this is impacting on your life today. When you become aware that your thinking, feelings and behaviours are frequently still linked to childhood, you can then make conscious choices to update these outdated scripts and live a more positive life.

Learn More
Reality Therapy

Explores how to look at your problem and identify what would be the best solution, therefore focus on what steps you can take to achieve same.

Learn More
Solution Focused Brief Therapy

Helps you identify and find ways to resolve a short term situation that is causing you concern.
